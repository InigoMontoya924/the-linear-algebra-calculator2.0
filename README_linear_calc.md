Thank you for using the linear algebra calculator. It is very simple to use and has quite strong functionality. In the application, by hovering on a textbox, the instruction bar at the bottom will display instructions. 

If you cannot open the app, or the app opens with errored functionalities and bugs(caused by incomplete import in some environments), use the “open” utility in the Terminal. Open the terminal app and enter “open ” and drag the app to the terminal window. Press enter and the app should open with full functionality. The app opening is very slow, so it might take a while.
